A Filmy Nature Backdrop

I love nature and just about everything about it. I love hiking, camping, swimming in lakes, flowers, rocks, the mountains, beaches, never ending plains, trees. You name it, and I probably love it (unless its a snake). But one of my most favorite things about nature is taking pictures of it. Especially with my film camera! And honestly, looking back through those pictures afterwards makes me just as happy- but in a nostalgic way. So come with me as we explore a little more into nature film photography- featuring my dinky little film camera that I love so dearly!

Here are just a few reasons why I love my film camera!

  1. It takes 1/2 frame photos - essentially meaning that it takes double the amount of photos per roll of film
  2. It's so portable!
    • Its light in build and small in size
    • It fits perfectly in small bags, yet is durable enough to be thrown in a bigger bag
    • The wrist strap makes it so you don't even need to bring it in a bag
  3. The film takes a few weeks to get back (this makes seeing the pictures even more exciting)
  4. It's just so cute!
  5. It takes amazing pictures
